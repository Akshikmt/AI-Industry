========================================================================
STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: CENTRIFUGAL WATER PUMP LUBRICATION (SOP-441)
========================================================================
Equipment Tag: P-101
Target System: Operations Coolant Loop
Department: Operations

1. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
- Bearing Type: Double-shielded deep groove ball bearing
- Lubricant Model: Mobilith SHC 220 Synthetic Grease
- Normal Operating Temperature: 45°C to 65°C
- Safety Alarm Limit (Thermal): 75°C
- Emergency Shutdown Limit (Thermal): 85°C

2. MAINTENANCE AND RE-GREASING INTERVALS
- Lubrication interval cycle: Every 150 operating hours.
- A weekly inspection log must be updated in the physical operations ledger.
- If bearing temperature sensor readings exceed 78°C, technicians must execute a lubricant viscosity test and schedule immediate grease replacement.

3. SEAL MAINTENANCE PROTOCOL
- Check mechanical seals daily for leakage.
- Normal leakage allowance: Less than 3 drops per minute.
- In the event of active leakage (exceeding 10 drops per minute), immediately deploy Seal Replacement Kit #P101-SK and notify the lead shift engineer.
