TECHNICAL MANUAL: RECIPROCATING AIR COMPRESSOR OPERATIONS (GUIDE-C303)
========================================================================
Equipment Tag: C-303
Target System: Instrument Air Utility Loop
Department: Safety / Maintenance

1. SYSTEM DESCRIPTION
The reciprocating compressor utilizes dual-stage piston cylinders to compress air to 8.5 bar for instrument valve controls.

2. MANDATORY OIL CHECK INTERVALS
- Crankcase oil level inspection: Every 24 hours of operation.
- Complete oil change cycle: Every 500 operating hours.
- Lubricant Spec: Mobil Rarus 827 Synthetic Reciprocating Compressor Oil.

3. OVERDUE PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE WARNINGS
If oil change is overdue by more than 10 operating hours, safety inspectors must issue a Warning ticket. Exceeding 25 hours overdue will trigger an automatic lockout on control panel logic to prevent crankcase seizure.
